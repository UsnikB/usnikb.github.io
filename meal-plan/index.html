Golden rules
Stop eating by 9:30 PM — non-negotiable for your 1 AM sleep
Drink 3–4L water daily. One large glass right after waking at 9 AM
Oil limit: 1–2 tsp per meal. Use a measuring spoon, not a ladle
1 cheat meal per week (Sunday biryani) — not a cheat day
Black coffee / green tea during fast hours (9 AM–1 PM)
10-min walk after each meal — massive help for desk workers
Midnight hunger: roasted makhana (20g) or curd only — ~80 kcal
Eat freely
Cucumber, tomato, cabbage, capsicum, spinach, mushroom
Lemon, lime, vinegar, all spices — no restriction
Green tea, black coffee (no sugar or milk)
Egg whites — add to any meal for near-zero calorie protein boost
Curd (1 cup per meal is fine — keeps digestion good)
All spice-based marinades — curd, chilli, ginger, garlic, jeera
Strictly limit
Chai with milk + sugar (3x daily = 300 hidden kcal)
Fruit juice, cold drinks, packaged snacks during weekdays
Rice — only on Sunday (biryani). Avoid rest of the week
Ghee / butter — 1 tsp max if you use it, never more
Alcohol — 150–200 empty kcal per drink, plan ahead
Late-night biscuits, chips, or anything easy to binge while coding
Airfryer cheat codes
Chicken breast: 190°C, 18 min. Thighs / legs: 200°C, 22 min
Fish fillets: 190°C, 12–14 min. Whole fish: 200°C, 18 min
Mutton chops: 190°C, 15 min + 2 min sear on tawa
Prawns: 180°C, 8 min (don't overcook — rubbery if you do)
Wings: pat DRY before seasoning — that's the crispy secret
Preheat airfryer 3 min before putting food in — always
